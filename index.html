The Good Foot

Look Good. Do Good.

Our Mission

We make bold, modern footwear for athletes while donating all of our profits to help those in need. We are part of Samaritan's Feet, a registered 503(c) non-profit organization that has donated millions of shoes to people around the world.

Our Designs

We design our footwear to be classically comfortable, with a bold, modern look.

They're also good for the Earth and support local manufacturing jobs.

Good Foot for Teams

Want to customize a design for your team?

We partner with coaches and athletes to show school spirit and spread the impact of our charities.
